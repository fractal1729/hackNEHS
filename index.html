About hackNEHS

We are throwing the most exciting hackathon in the New England area, exclusively for high school students. It will be a day full of developing cool ideas, meeting other high school hackers, and learning the ins and outs of development.

Highschool hackathons are abundant in the west coast/California area, but are lacking on the other side of the country. hackNEHS is designed to allow high schoolers in the New England area to experience a hackathon. We want to introduce to beginners an easy way to code and spark their interests, or give more experienced coders a chance to explore new ideas and technology.

Hackathons are events in which participants fully build a software project from scratch. They have become popular in tech giants, startups, and schools alike because they enable hackers to bring their ideas to life while having fun. At hackNEHS, we will have many bright high school hackers working in teams to build something awesome, with experienced mentors providing guidance along the way. We will also have hands-on workshops for novices to learn to use the hottest technologies of today, including iOS programming and web development.

FAQ

I don't know how to code. Can I still come?

Whether you're an experienced hacker or you've never written a line of code before, you are welcome at hackNEHS. Going to a hackathon like hackNEHS is a fantastic way to learn coding -- you’ll be able to learn everything you need to know during morning workshops, and then you can get hacking in the afternoon. If you’re already well-versed in coding, then you can jump straight into hacking, skipping the workshops.

What should I bring?

A computer, its charger, and a valid school ID are all you need. Pillows and warm clothing are recommended. Attendance is completely free, and food and drinks will be provided!

Can I be a mentor?

Of course! Contact us at info@hacknehs.com.

What if I don't have a team right now?

Don’t worry. You will have an opportunity to find a team on the day of the hackathon.

How much does attendance cost?

It’s free! Thanks to our generous sponsors, you will be able to attend hackNEHS with no charge whatsoever.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event where like-minded computer enthusiasts meet to work together on computer programming. Teams collaborate to build and code something original from websites and apps to machines and robots.

Will food be provided?

Yes, food will be provided. We do not currently know specifically what we will serve, but we assure you it will be delicious. Let us know if you have any dietary restrictions -- we’ll have you covered too.

How does scoring work?

One hack or project are to be submitted by each team to qualify for demos and prizes. Experienced judges will critique each submission based on functionability, creativity, and technical difficulty.

Who can attend?

hackNEHS is open to all high schoolers. If you’re in middle school and would like to attend, shoot us an email at info@hacknehs.com.

How do teams work?

A team usually consists of around 4-5 people who work together on a project for the entire event. You can come with a team of old friends, or make one with new friends you meet at the event!

What are workshops?

Workshops will be small, interactive classes teaching novices how to code through hands-on coding activities. They will give participants the basic tools necessary to build something cool in the hackathon. Workshops will be offered on various tools, including iOS programming skills and web development. Anyone interested in learning can sign up for one, or even more!

Will you provide transportation?

If there are enough people from your school attending hackNEHS, we can get a bus to bring you to the event, so get your friends and classmates to come! That said, you can also opt to drive to the event.
